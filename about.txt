Medium description:

Long description:
Sidqly is a donation transparency and operations platform for mosques, Islamic charities, NGOs, and donors. It is being built to help organizations manage donation tracking, payment proof review, approval workflows, campaign operations, vendor and field proof, donor reporting, and impact transparency across Islamic giving workflows such as Zakat, Sadaqah, Qurbani, Ramadan campaigns, and Aqiqah.

Core workflows:
Donation tracking
Payment proof review
Approval workflows
Qurbani campaign tracking
Zakat management
Sadaqah campaign management
Ramadan donation campaigns
Aqiqah workflows
Donor reporting
Vendor and field task tracking
Proof upload and approval
Organization dashboards
Role-based access
Audit trail design
Recipient privacy focus
Impact reporting

Trust principles:
Payment proof should be reviewed before approval.
Proof should be approved by authorized roles.
Donor transparency should be improved.
Recipient privacy should be protected.
Organizations should remain separated.
Role-based access should control sensitive actions.
Audit trails should support accountability.
Reports should help organizations explain impact.
