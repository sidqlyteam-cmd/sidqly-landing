The problem Sidqly solves

Many donation teams manage payment screenshots, receipts, donor updates, field proof, and reports through WhatsApp, spreadsheets, and manual follow-ups. Sidqly brings these workflows into one organized system.

How Sidqly works

1. Donor gives

Donors support campaigns like Zakat, Sadaqah, Qurbani, Ramadan, or general charity.

2. Payment is reviewed

Payment proof can be checked by authorized finance or admin roles before approval.

3. Work is tracked

Teams, vendors, or field staff complete assigned tasks and upload proof where needed.

4. Donors see impact

Approved proof, reports, and updates help donors understand where their contribution went.

FAQ

What is Sidqly?

Sidqly is a donation transparency platform for mosques, Islamic charities, NGOs, and donors.

Who is Sidqly for?

It is built for mosques, charities, NGOs, Qurbani organizers, Zakat teams, finance officers, vendors, field teams, and donors.

Does Sidqly auto-approve payment proof?

No. Sidqly is being designed so payment proof is reviewed by authorized roles before approval.
