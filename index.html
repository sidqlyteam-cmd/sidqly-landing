The problem Sidqly solves

Many mosques and charities still manage donation screenshots, receipts, donor updates, field proof, campaign status, and reports through WhatsApp, spreadsheets, and manual follow-ups. Sidqly is being built to bring these workflows into one organized and transparent system.

How Sidqly works

1. Donor gives

Donors support campaigns such as Zakat, Sadaqah, Qurbani, Ramadan drives, Aqiqah, or general charity.

2. Payment is reviewed

Payment proof can be reviewed by authorized finance or admin roles before the donation is marked as approved.

3. Work is tracked

Organization teams, vendors, volunteers, or field staff can complete assigned tasks and upload proof where needed.

4. Donors see impact

Approved proof, reports, and campaign updates help donors understand how their contribution was used.

Designed around trust, privacy, and proof

Sidqly is being designed with payment review, proof approval, organization separation, role-based access, donor transparency, audit trails, and recipient privacy at the center. The goal is to help organizations reduce manual stress while increasing donor confidence.

Donation transparency platform for Pakistan and beyond

Sidqly is starting with the needs of mosques, Islamic charities, NGOs, and donation teams in Pakistan, with a long-term vision to support transparent giving workflows globally. The platform is focused on practical donation operations: collecting support, reviewing payment proof, assigning work, tracking fulfillment, approving proof, and reporting impact.

FAQ

What is Sidqly?

Sidqly is a donation transparency platform for mosques, Islamic charities, NGOs, and donors. It helps teams manage donation proof, approvals, campaigns, reports, and donor trust.

Who is Sidqly for?

Sidqly is built for mosques, charities, NGOs, Qurbani organizers, Zakat teams, finance officers, vendors, field teams, volunteers, and donors.

Does Sidqly auto-approve payment proof?

No. Sidqly is being designed so payment proof is reviewed by authorized roles before approval.

Can Sidqly support Qurbani, Zakat, Sadaqah, and Ramadan campaigns?

Yes. Sidqly is being designed for Islamic donation workflows including Qurbani, Zakat, Sadaqah, Ramadan campaigns, Aqiqah, and general charity operations.

Is the full Sidqly app live?

Sidqly is currently preparing early pilot access. The public website explains the platform, while the full app is being developed privately for controlled testing and launch.
